You can download Instagram and YouTube videos. We provide the original resolution as uploaded by the creator, so you might not be able to download all resolutions. What you download is exactly what was uploaded

--- How to Download ---

Copy the Link

Open the Instagram or YouTube app/website, and copy the URL of the video you want to download

Paste the Link

Return to the Elite Downloader website, paste the copied URL into the input field.

Choose the Platform

Select whether the link is from Instagram or YouTube in the platform selection area

Show Resolutions

Click the "Show Resolution" button to retrieve available download options based on the platform.

Choose Format

  • Audio Only
  • Video Only (with selected resolution)
  • Audio and Video (with selected resolution)

Download

After selecting your format, click the "Download" button to start your download.
